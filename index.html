Carlos Iñiguez is a Professor in informatics at Escuela Politécnica Nacional, Ecuador. He receives his Ph.D. from Universitat Politècnica de València (Spain) in 2019 where he was a member of the Genomic Group at PROS Research Centre. He holds a Master Degree on Web Engineering (2013) from Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (Spain) and a University Degree in System Engineering and Computation (2005) from Universidad Politécnia Nacional (Ecuador). Additionally, he has carried out music studies at Conservatorio Nacional de Música (Ecuador) in violin interpretation. He has 7 years of professional experience, having worked in several private and public companies from Ecuador. He is currently involved on the design of web-based user interfaces for extracting knowledge from the genomic domain.
