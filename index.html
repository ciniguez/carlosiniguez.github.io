Shortbio

Carlos Iñiguez is a Professor in informatics at Escuela Politécnica Nacional, Ecuador. He receives his Ph.D. from Universitat Politècnica de València (Spain) in 2019 where he was a member of the Genomic Group at PROS Research Centre. He holds a Master Degree on Web Engineering (2013) from Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (Spain) and a University Degree in System Engineering and Computation (2005) from Universidad Politécnia Nacional (Ecuador). Additionally, he has carried out music studies at Conservatorio Nacional de Música (Ecuador) in violin interpretation. He has many| years of professional experience, having worked in several private and public companies from Ecuador. He is currently involved on the design of web-based user interfaces, Development mthodologies and software development.

Industry Experience

Carlos is an IT professional with more than 17 years of experience in software development, project management, and technological innovation. Since 2014, I have worked as a Full-Time Assistant Professor at the National Polytechnic University of Ecuador, where I contribute to the training of future systems engineers and actively participate in academic committees aimed at curricular updating, graduate monitoring, and international accreditation. In the business field, I have led strategic technological projects in key institutions. At SUPERCOM (2014), I coordinated and advised the implementation of systems in the National Directorate of Development and Management of Platforms. As Software Development Leader at the Bupartech - BitAhead Consortium (2013), I managed the reengineering of the National Payment System of the Central Bank of Ecuador, an essential project for the national financial system. My experience at the Ministry of Tourism of Ecuador (2006–2012) is highlighted by the coordination of innovative projects, such as the e-REGItur, the GEOPORTAL of Tourist Cadastre and a mobile application for updating tourist establishments. I also led the implementation of the SIPLANEO operational planning system and developed the corporate intranet and technical support systems, improving institutional operational efficiency. In addition, I coordinated the implementation of business intelligence platforms (PENTAHOO) and document management (ALFRESCO). Throughout my career, I have combined technical and leadership skills to drive technological innovation, optimize institutional processes and coordinate multidisciplinary teams in high-impact projects.
